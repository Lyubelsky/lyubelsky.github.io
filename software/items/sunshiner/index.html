Sunshiner


$1.00

This is a sunlight generator that adds realistic sunlight to dull/grayish images that have almost no natural sunlight on them, and makes them shine!

Sunshiner also automatically detects the direction the sunlight should be generated from based on the image so that it blends perfectly real when generating!

Click the "Upload Image" button, then adjust the "Strength" of the sunlight and the "Threshold" for surfaces to be considered "Where sunlight touches", click the image to toggle seeing the difference between the original image and the sunlit image, and then when you are pleased with the results, hit the "Save Image" button and use your image for any purpose!
