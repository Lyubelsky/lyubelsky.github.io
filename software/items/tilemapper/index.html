Tilemapper is a quick tool to turn multiple images into a single tilemap image.

Click the 'Upload Tiles' button and select all single image tiles that you want to combine. Then, after loading the images, you will see a preview of your tilemap. Then, click the 'Save Tilemap' button to save your tilemap as a .png image!
